Turkey and Cheese Sandwich

Description

An easy and tasty snack to take on the go

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole wheat bread
  • 20g of turkey breast roast (cooked)
  • 1 slice of low fat cheese
  • 1/4 cup of baby spinach (optional)
  • 1 thinly sliced raddit (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all of your ingredients
  2. Lay your bread on a clean surface and place the slice of turkey on it
  3. Add your slice of low fat cheese
  4. Add your spinach (optional)
  5. Add your raddish (optional)
  6. Place the second slice of bread on top and enjoy
